Our Core Purpose

Vision & Mission

Driving educational excellence, social upliftment, and holistic development.

Thiruvalluvar College was established in 1968 by our founder Sri-la-Sri Deviasigamani Arunachala Desiga Paramachariya Swamigal (Thavathiru Kundrakudi Adigalar) offering both UG and PG programmes affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli.

Our Vision

To upliftment the people socially and economically in the rural area through which the seeds of inclusion of quality education are sown.

Our Mission

Humility & Trust Committed to serving society with humility and trust, devoid of exploitation, to impart value-based education, particularly to socially and economically deprived sections of the rural area; to make the students of this institution worthy citizens of our glorious motherland.

Scientific Zeal To instill scientific zeal and develop skilled human resources to contemporary challenges.

Social Concern To sensitize learners towards inclusive social concerns, human rights, gender, and environmental issues.

Empowerment To empower economically and socially disadvantaged and underprivileged rural students and equip them to shine in their academic skills and to have rich moral value.

The mission in its most succinct form is enshrined in the motto’s for goal, it is explicitly stated in the College calendar. As for the other goals and objectives, each of them is a recognizable part of our College tradition, frequently talked about in our meetings and implicitly expressed in our policies, practices and activities.
