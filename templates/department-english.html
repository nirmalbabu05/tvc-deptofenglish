Unaided Course

Department of English

To strive, to seek, to find, and not to yield. Fostering linguistic excellence and literary passion since inception.

Course Objectives

To facilitate students enrich their communication skills in the English language and inculcate in them a love for reading and interpreting literature.

To enrich the aesthetic sensibility and critical acumen of the students by exposing them to good literature from around the world.

To help them understand the nuances of life through literature and relate to fellow human beings meaningfully.

Providing space for optimum growth of latent talents, the department continues ‘to strive, to seek, to find, and not to yield.'

A candidate who has passed in the higher secondary examinations of Tamilnadu higher secondary board or some other board accepted by the syndicate as equivalent can apply.
