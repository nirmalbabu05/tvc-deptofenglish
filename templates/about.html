College Profile

Discover our legacy, values, and the vibrant academic community that has shaped futures since 1968.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

The College started functioning in the year 1968-69. It is named after the great THIRUVALLUVAR.

It is very close to the temple dedicated to “Lord Siva” at Papanasam (Tirunelveli District) on the banks of river Thamiraparani in sylvan surroundings contributed by the Pothigai, supposed to be the seat of Agasthiyar, who nurtured the Tamil language in all its varied excellence, thousands of years ago.

The College is under the benign management of Kundrakkudi Thiruvannamalai Adheenam Trust headed by His Holiness Srimath Ponnambala Adigalar.

Inspiring Leadership

Under the inspiring leadership, wise guidance and loving care of Late “His Holiness Kundrakudi Adigalar”, Founder President and former Secretary, the college achieved all-round development. His vision continues to light the path of our institution towards educational excellence and social upliftment.
