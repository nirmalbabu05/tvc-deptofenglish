Nature & Environment

Eco Club

Promoting, monitoring and operating environmental activities for a greener tomorrow and sustainable college ecosystem.

Coordinator

Tmt. S. Kannaki

Asst. Prof of Tamil

Ecoclub aims to promote, monitor and operate the environmental activities of the college and neighborhood communities for the sound environmental behavior. We believe in creating a sustainable and green campus for future generations.

Objectives of the Club

01

To create awareness about the ecological degradation and the need for eco system protection among students.

02

To create awareness on environmental issues and highlights basic health and hygiene care among the public.

03

To encourage students to undertake mini projects on economic impacts of environmental pollution.

04

To plant and maintain saplings in places close to the college and to ensure the region a Plastic Free Zone.

The mission in its most succinct form is enshrined in the motto’s for goal, it is explicitly stated in the College calendar. As for the other goals and objectives, each of them is a recognizable part of our College tradition, frequently talked about in our meetings and implicitly expressed in our policies, practices and activities.
