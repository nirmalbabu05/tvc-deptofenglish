Alumni Association

Our Mission

To inform, to engage and to inspire the reputed alumni to stay connected to their alma mater, to support its vision and to contribute to its constant victory.

Core Objectives

To help and promote the welfare of present learners.

To assist the students for employment opportunities.

To develop the infrastructure amenities of the college.

To conduct coaching classes for competitive examinations.

To enable the poor and best students for awards and prizes.

To assist students in sports and forward them to state level competitions.

To organize industrial training for the students.

The mission in its most succinct form is enshrined in the motto’s for goal, it is explicitly stated in the College calendar. As for the other goals and objectives, each of them is a recognizable part of our College tradition, frequently talked about in our meetings and implicitly expressed in our policies, practices and activities.
